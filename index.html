About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at the University of Technology Sydney (UTS), supervised by Prof. Min Xu. Prior to joining UTS, I earned my Master degree in Communications Engineering from The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen and my B.Eng. in Electronic Information Science and Technology from Sun Yat-sen University.

My research focuses on mmWave sensing, Wi-Fi sensing, and Large Language Model applications.
